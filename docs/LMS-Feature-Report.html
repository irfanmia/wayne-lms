1 Executive Summary

Wayne LMS LMS is a modern, full-stack learning management system purpose-built for the Middle East and Dubai market. The platform targets aspiring and professional developers with a comprehensive suite of interactive courses, hands-on coding exercises, quizzes, assignments, and gamified learning experiences.

Mission: To democratize coding education across the MENA region by delivering a world-class, Arabic-supported, mobile-first learning platform that rivals global competitors like Udemy, Coursera, and Exercism — while being locally relevant and culturally aware.

The platform is fully operational with 10 published courses, 12 coding tracks, a complete admin dashboard, and student-facing features including certificates, gamification, and multi-language support (English, Arabic RTL, Spanish).
